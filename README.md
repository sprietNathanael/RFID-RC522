RFID-RC522
==========

Codes utiles pour le module RFID-RC522

| Pins | SPI      | UNO  | Mega2560 | Leonardo |
| ---- |:--------:|:----:|:--------:|:--------:|
| 1    | SDA (SS) |  10  |  53      | 10       |
| 2    | SCK      |  13  |  52      | SCK`1`   |
| 3    | MOSI     |  11  |  51      | MOSI`1`  |
| 4    | MISO     |  12  |  50      | MISO`1`  |
| 5    | IRQ      |  `*` |  `*`     | `*`      |
| 6    | GND      |  GND |  GND     | GND      |
| 7    | RST      |  5   |  ?       | Reset    |
| 8    | +3.3V    |  3V3 |  3V3     | 3.3V     |
`*` Pas obligatoire 
`1` header ICPS
